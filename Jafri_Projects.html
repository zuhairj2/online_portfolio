Lost Survival Adventure

September 2017, Computer Science and Engineering

The objective of this project was to make a game or story from scratch . My partner, Danny Jung and I made a story that involves some game aspects to it. The plot of the story is about a character who is stranded on an island and he does not know how he got on it in the first place. You as the conscious or the voice of reason leads this character through the islands, meeting strange people and trying to get off the island. This story is very interesting with nice mechanics and sprites and my partner and I worked very hard on this project.

Image Manipulation Project

March 2018, Computer Science and Engineering

Click here to see our Project Notebook!

The objective of this project was to create a frame for a family by manipulating an image to look like it. My partner Zachary Chew and I made programs to add a border to the image so that it looks like the image has been framed. We created three different types of frames, a rounded frame, a circular frame and a frame that has different colors on the side. I enjoyed working on this project with my partner because it was very exciting working and creating new images with our imagination.
